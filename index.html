The Quiz

Here's how it works:

I show you German words, and you have to translate them into English. Do it fast, do it right, and you score points. Mess up, and I'll grin while your opponent takes the lead.

Play alone if you dare, or challenge a friend. In multiplayer, I throw in jokers — surprise tricks that can change the game in an instant. Because learning words should feel like an adventure, not homework.

So step into the arena. Collect points. Beat your rivals. And remember: llamas never forget who wins… or who loses.
